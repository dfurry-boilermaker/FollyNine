Trading

Over the past few years, I have become fascinated with the trading world. This has led me down a path of studying the psychology of trading, risk management, and technical analysis. I have learned the most while reading books like Market Wizards by Jack D. Schwager, Technical Analysis of the Financial Markets by John J. Murphy, and Martin Zweig's Winning on Wall Street. I have found that trading is a great way to make money, but it is also an even greater way to lose money. I am striving to improve my trade strategy and materialize my conviction continuously. I have documented some of my analysis publicly on the FollyNine X profile; check it out by clicking on the X logo!

noun · /ˈfälē/

1. lack of good sense; foolishness.
"an act of sheer folly"

Developed by Daniel Furry

You might wonder why anyone would choose "folly" to describe their website. Well, I'm glad you asked… Folly perfectly captures the essence of this site. In the past, folly has been a term to affectionately describe things like family cars, sailboats, and even my childhood puppy, a Black Lab Mutt with only three legs. At first glance, these things might seem silly or even foolish, as the definition suggests. But in reality, they've been the very things that have brought my family and friends together time and time again, creating unforgettable memories that we'll cherish forever.
